## 5. Adding Taxonomy Fields

You can also add custom metadata to your category or tag pages. The process is very similar to adding fields to Post Types, but instead of Meta Boxes, you use **Sections**.

1. **Add a Section**: Click the **Add Section** button located at the top right of the Edit Term Meta Fields screen.
2. **Configure Section**: Enter the details for your new Section in the settings panel (e.g., Field Name and Label).
3. **Add Fields**: Click the **+** button inside the created Section in the List View. Select the desired field from the modal that appears.
4. **Save**: Click **Save Changes** at the bottom of the screen to apply your taxonomy fields.
